Who is Tazmane?

The question of who we are arises often. I am a mixture of my influences, my surroundings and my life experiences.

Bubbly by nature, driven by choice and extremely attentive. I have a strong personality and tend to lead however I strongly believe that we are stronger together than alone.

I spent three years of my life living in a boarding, the school I attended was extremely different to what I was used to. Its sole purpose was largely to give children that showed potential in math and science an opportunity to access a very good education. Especially to those whose parents were not in a position to afford it.

Those three years made me a lot less dependent on my parents and friends, it really challenged my understanding of the world. In doing so it allowed me to question a lot and also become more assure of who I am. It taught me patients, empathy, tolerance and understand because those in conjunction with honestly and mainly respect allowing for really good interpersonal skills and conflict resolution.


Most importantly exposed me to the world of Information Technology.
